# Aurum Circuit – Ethical Brand Standards

The Aurum Circuit is more than a technology — it is a philosophy.  
These standards ensure the brand reflects its core values.

## Human-First Priority
All mentions, visuals, or uses must center human wellbeing.

## No Fear, No Force
The brand cannot appear beside violent, aggressive, fear-based, or abusive contexts.

## Dignity-Driven Presentation
Imagery must emphasize:
- softness
- safety
- care
- support
- natural movement

## Truthfulness
No exaggerated claims, false medical promises, or deceptive marketing.

## Inclusivity
Aurum Circuit materials must welcome all people regardless of ability, background, or status.
