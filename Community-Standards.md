# Aurum Circuit – Community & Partnership Rules

## For Partners
Potential partners must:
- respect human-first ethics
- avoid exploitative goals
- agree to non-harm principles
- maintain transparent communication

## For Collaborators
Contributors must:
- document their work honestly
- follow the Code of Conduct
- never claim your work as their own
- acknowledge the project's origin

## No Corporate Exploitation
Partnerships cannot:
- privatize shared work
- commercialize the project without approval
- undermine the brand’s ethics
