The Aurum Circuit is an original human-first technology philosophy and design framework created by Danielle Austin to support safety, mobility, and dignity through soft, biomimetic materials and ethical innovation principles.

This Master Policy governs the use, representation, protection, and stewardship of the Aurum Circuit brand, identity, ideas, research, prototypes, and ecosystem.
It overrides and anchors all sub-policies contained within this repository.

⸻

1. Brand Definition

The Aurum Circuit represents:
	•	A human-first approach to future mobility and support
	•	Soft-technology materials that protect without restricting
	•	Biomimetic tendon-based reinforcement patterns
	•	Ethical innovation that reduces strain, not increases it
	•	A zero-harm, dignity-first design philosophy

It is both a creative technology framework and a protected intellectual ecosystem.

5. Ethical Alignment Requirements

All use or discussion of the Aurum Circuit must follow the No-Harm Charter, including:
	•	Do not develop or encourage harmful technologies
	•	Respect human autonomy and dignity
	•	Prioritise safety, mobility, rehabilitation and support
	•	Maintain transparency in any collaborative work
	•	Never weaponise or distort the philosophy for profit or exploitation

Any misaligned use automatically violates this policy.
