FUTURE LICENSING FRAMEWORK

For researchers, labs, hospitals, universities, or innovation partners

⸻

Tier 1 — Research License (Non-Commercial)

Allows:
	•	Reading, referencing
	•	Academic collaboration
	•	Prototype testing under supervision

Does NOT allow:
	•	Selling
	•	Branding
	•	Modification without approval

⸻

Tier 2 — Limited Commercial License

Allows:
	•	Joint development
	•	Prototyping
	•	Manufacturing trials
	•	Revenue sharing

⸻

Tier 3 — Exclusive Strategic Partnership

Reserved for:
	•	Medical tech companies
	•	Soft robotics labs
	•	Regenerative material R&D groups
	•	Sports medicine innovation hubs

Includes:
	•	Full co-development
	•	Shared patents
	•	Licensing royalty agreement
	•	NDA
